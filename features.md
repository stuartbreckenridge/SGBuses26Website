Discover Singapore Buses: Your Ultimate Bus Companion

Welcome to the future of bus commuting in Singapore! Singapore Buses offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to elevate your daily journey:

1. Real-Time Bus Insights

Stay ahead of the curve with precise* bus arrival predictions, capacity updates, vehicle types, and detailed bus schedules—all at your fingertips.

2. Interactive Map Exploration

Experience the magic of our interactive map! Drag to explore and watch as bus stops smoothly materialise before your eyes. Tap any stop to uncover additional details or check for upcoming arrivals.

3. Swift and Smart Search

Find your way effortlessly by searching for bus stops via code, name, or road, and routes by their service numbers. Results appear in real-time as you type, ensuring a seamless experience.

4. Favorites for Quick Access

Mark your most-used bus stops as favourites, ensuring swift and hassle-free access whenever needed.

5. Route Revelation

Discover the remaining stops on your selected bus route, all from the bus stop you're currently viewing. Want the bigger picture? Tap "View Route Map" to visualise the entire route. Plus, our map annotations convey the bus's direction intuitively.

6. Notify Me on Arrival

No more wondering when to disembark! Activate "Notify Me on Arrival", and the app will notify you promptly as you approach your chosen bus stop.

7. Explore Your Surroundings with "Look Around"

Explore the vicinity of a bus stop to understand your surroundings better.

8. Live Activities (Singapore Buses+ Required)

A next-generation tracking feature exclusive to Singapore Buses+: track upcoming arrivals right from your Lock Screen. No more constant app-opening—stay informed effortlessly.

9. Works Offline

Even when you're offline, our app remains your trusty companion. While you won't receive real-time arrival estimates, route and schedule information remains accessible.

Embark on a smoother, smarter, and more convenient bus journey with Singapore Buses. Download now and unlock a world of transit possibilities!

* As precise as the data provided.
